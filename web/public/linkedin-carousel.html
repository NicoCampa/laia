Where to use them
Applications

Use local models where constraints are the feature.

The best use cases are private, repetitive, bounded, and easy to verify.

01Private summarizationNotes, PDFs, transcripts, logs, and personal knowledge bases.
02Classification & routingTag emails, tickets, snippets, files, or records before a human review.
03ExtractionTurn repeated document patterns into structured fields.
04Coding helpersLocal autocomplete, refactor hints, tests, and small scripts.
05Offline agentsRun simple tool workflows without a constant network dependency.
06Embedded appsPut language features closer to the user and device.
Use local whenData sensitivity, latency, cost, or offline access matters.
Use cloud whenThe task needs frontier reasoning, high stakes, or broad generality.
Choose by task, not by hype cycle. 09 / 10
